# Capability 3: Transformation Planning

**Domain:** Architecture Development – Common

**Tagline:** Aligning with business goals and minimizing disruption through a structured and coordinated approach.

## Definition

Transformation Planning involves developing a comprehensive plan to transition from the current state to the target architecture. It includes identifying the necessary steps, resources, and timelines to achieve the desired transformation.

## Objectives

Translate the architecture roadmap into actionable transformation plans. Define work packages, dependencies, resource needs and governance to enable controlled execution with minimal disruption.

## Tasks

1. **Dependency analysis:** identify cross-project dependencies, risks and sequencing constraints.
2. **Work package definition:** break roadmap initiatives into actionable work packages with owners.
3. **Transition architecture design:** define intermediate states between current and target.
4. **Resource and budget allocation:** assign resources, budget and timelines per work package.
5. **Stakeholder alignment:** validate and approve the transformation plan with key stakeholders.

## Inputs

- Architecture Roadmap
- Business strategy and goals
- Resource availabilities (budget, people, technology)
- Risk assessments and dependency information

## Outputs

- Transformation Plan document with work packages
- Transition architecture models
- Resource and budget allocation plan
- Risk and dependency register

## Roles

- Business Architect, Application Architect, Data Architect, Technology Architect (R)
- Lead Enterprise Architect (A)

## Common Issues with an Immature Capability

- "Rogue" projects that are not in line with the overall strategy and enterprise architecture
- Unrealistic timelines (overly ambitious) and project timelines that are not aligned to each other
- Scope creep and unclear prioritization

## Pragmatic Approach – Essentials That Are Needed for Success

- Align the project portfolio on strategic objectives of the projects
- Define high-level priorities across projects / initiatives
- Setup a holistic risk management across all projects / initiatives
- Define clear governance for transformation decisions and change requests
