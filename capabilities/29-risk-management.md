# Capability 29: Risk Management

**Domain:** Governance

## Description

Identify, assess, mitigate, and monitor architecture-related risks across the enterprise. This capability ensures that architecture decisions account for risk and that the organization is resilient to technology and architecture failures.

## Objectives

- Identify and assess architecture-related risks proactively
- Define and implement risk mitigation strategies
- Integrate architecture risk into enterprise risk management
- Ensure architecture resilience and business continuity

## Key Activities

- Architecture risk identification and cataloging
- Risk assessment and scoring (likelihood, impact)
- Risk mitigation strategy development
- Technical debt risk assessment
- Vendor and technology risk evaluation
- Architecture risk monitoring and reporting
- Risk appetite and tolerance definition
- Business continuity and resilience architecture
- Integration with enterprise risk management (ERM)

## Inputs

- Architecture assessments and reviews
- Technical debt register
- Vendor and technology assessments
- Business continuity requirements
- Compliance and audit findings
- Industry threat intelligence
- Current state assessment results

## Outputs / Deliverables

- Architecture risk register
- Risk assessment reports
- Risk mitigation plans
- Risk heat maps and dashboards
- Risk trend reports
- Business continuity architecture requirements
- Risk appetite statements

## Maturity Indicators

| Level | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Initial | Risks identified reactively after incidents |
| Developing | Basic risk register maintained; ad-hoc assessments |
| Defined | Formal risk management process; regular assessment cycles |
| Managed | Risk actively monitored; integrated with enterprise risk management |
| Optimizing | Predictive risk analysis; proactive risk management; resilience by design |

## Key Roles

- Enterprise Architect
- Risk Manager
- Security Architect
- Architecture Review Board
- Business Continuity Manager
- Chief Architect

## Tools & Techniques

- Risk assessment frameworks (ISO 31000, NIST RMF)
- Risk registers and heat maps
- GRC (Governance, Risk, Compliance) tools
- Technical debt analysis tools
- Scenario planning and stress testing
- Monte Carlo simulation

## KPIs / Metrics

- Number of identified architecture risks
- Risk exposure (aggregate risk score)
- Risk mitigation plan completion rate
- Number of risk-related incidents
- Mean time to mitigate risks
- Technical debt risk score

## Relationships

- **Current State Assessment** - Baseline assessment identifies risks
- **Security Architecture** - Security risks are architecture risks
- **Compliance Management** - Non-compliance creates risk
- **Infrastructure Architecture** - Infrastructure resilience mitigates risk
- **Change Management** - Changes introduce or mitigate risks
