# Capability 25: Innovation & Emerging Technology

**Domain:** Architecture Management

## Description

Scout, evaluate, and integrate emerging technologies and innovation into the enterprise architecture. This capability ensures the organization stays informed about technology trends and can strategically adopt those that create value.

## Objectives

- Monitor and assess emerging technology trends
- Evaluate the applicability and impact of new technologies
- Guide strategic technology adoption decisions
- Foster a culture of innovation and experimentation

## Key Activities

- Technology trend monitoring and scanning
- Emerging technology evaluation and proof of concept
- Technology radar maintenance
- Innovation lab / sandbox management
- Vendor and startup engagement
- Technology impact assessment
- Innovation pipeline management
- Cross-industry learning and benchmarking
- Experimental architecture design

## Inputs

- Industry analyst reports and research
- Technology vendor roadmaps
- Startup and innovation ecosystem insights
- Business strategy and innovation goals
- Internal innovation ideas
- Conference and community learnings

## Outputs / Deliverables

- Technology radar / trend reports
- Technology evaluation reports
- Proof of concept results
- Innovation recommendations
- Technology adoption roadmaps
- Innovation pipeline / portfolio
- Emerging technology guidelines

## Maturity Indicators

| Level | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Initial | No systematic technology monitoring; reactive adoption |
| Developing | Ad-hoc technology exploration; occasional proof of concepts |
| Defined | Technology radar maintained; regular evaluation cycles |
| Managed | Innovation pipeline active; PoCs systematically managed |
| Optimizing | Innovation embedded in culture; strategic technology leadership |

## Key Roles

- Chief Technology Officer (CTO)
- Enterprise Architect
- Innovation Lead / Manager
- Technology Strategist
- Solution Architects
- R&D teams

## Tools & Techniques

- Technology Radar (ThoughtWorks model)
- Gartner Hype Cycle and Magic Quadrant
- Proof of concept / pilot frameworks
- Innovation frameworks (design thinking, lean startup)
- Hackathons and innovation sprints
- Vendor briefings and demos

## KPIs / Metrics

- Number of technologies evaluated per period
- Proof of concept completion rate
- Technologies successfully adopted
- Innovation pipeline value
- Time from technology identification to adoption decision
- Employee engagement in innovation activities

## Relationships

- **Standards & Reference Architectures** - Emerging tech feeds standards evolution
- **Target State Design** - Innovation influences target state
- **Architecture Skills Development** - New technologies require new skills
- **Platform Architecture** - New platforms emerge from innovation
- **Data Integration & Analytics** - AI/ML and new analytics capabilities
